Behind Free Range Factory

I love electrical engineering things, I love programming and I love to make stuff that can fly, normally under the form of a drone or something.

My name is Fabrizio, I am an engineer and I live in Barcelona, Spain. Since I was young I have always liked to build things. Growing up I decided to keep doing it so after a lots of effort to get an electrical engineering PhD from this Australian university I spent many years working as scientist and wrote some research papers.

I am the co-author of the programming book Free Range VHDL and the co-author of few other books. I've built and maintain this massive VHDL database and I dream about building more and more things. I've built this website to share some of the stuff I like.

Please have a look around and if you would like to communicate with me please reach out to me.
