Behing Free Range Factory

I love electrical engineering things, I love programming and I love to make stuff that can fly, normally under the form of a drone or something.

My name is Fabrizio, I am an engineer and I live in Barcelona, Spain. Since I was young I have always liked to build things. Growing up I decided to keep doing it so after a lots of effort to get an electrical engineering PhD from this Australian university I spent many years of my life working as scienties and wrote lots of research papers. My nature and my work have taken me to live in and travell all over the World: Japan, Switzerland, Australia and now Spain.

I am the co-author of the programming book Free Range VHDL and the co-author of few other books. I've built and maintain this massive VHDL database and I dream about building more and more things. I've built this website to make some of it public.

Please have a look around and if you would like to communicate with me please reach out to me.
