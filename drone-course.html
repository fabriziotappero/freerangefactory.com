Are you into electronics? Build a drones is a good and practical way to get better at it. This course is designed to explore in great details how to make your own drone.

Let's step away from what the current consumer market can offer, learn how a drone is made and learn how to build and fly your own designed drone.

In this course you will learn the mechanics and the electronics needed to build your own unique drone. From a cool light-weight acrobatic quadcopter to a funky tricopter specifically designed for shooting ultra-stable video footage.

To successfully complete this course you do not need any university degree. A great desire of learning electronics and mechanics is all it's requiered.

CLASS CURRICULUM

LEVEL A
  • Introduction to drone technology
  • Build a quadcopter frame
  • Make laser-cut drone parts for fast prototyping
  • Design fiber-reinforced 3D printed parts
  • Drone fundamental electronics
  • ARM-based flight controller
  • Brushless motor electronics speed controller
  • Radio communication and telemetry
  • GPS/GNSS receivers and navigation
  • Fly a quadcopter
LEVEL B

Let's build upon the experience aquired in level A video course to make unique and challanging stuff.

  • Desing and make your custom-made drone
  • Build a wireless communication link
  • Embedded ARM C++ programming for a drone
  • Measure on-board vibrations in real-time
  • Build a vibration-free camera gimbal
  • GPS postioning and relative displacement with on-board IMU
  • Autonomous flight
  • Drone consuption and long-lasting battery
