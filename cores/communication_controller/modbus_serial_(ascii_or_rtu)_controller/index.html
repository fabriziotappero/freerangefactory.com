Description

This controller is targetting fpga based applications were a microcontroller is not needed or wanted.
By design everything will be based on state machines.
No consideration will be done on power efficiency at the initial stage.
Currently on the drawing board for implementation.
Functions available are to be defined by use of generics as most of the people only use a narrow subset of all functions.
