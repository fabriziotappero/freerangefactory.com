Description

ISO/IEC14443 A is an important to the finance identification smartcard and NFC ASIC. And the modified Miller decoder is the key module for the wireless / dual-interface smartcard ASIC implementation. There are four data speed for ISO14443 A (2005 version), ie:
* fc/128, or 106kbps, the default downlink data speed;
* fc/64 (212kbps), fc/32 (424kbps) and fc/16 (847kbps), high speed for data exchange between PCD and PICC;
The mmiller_decod106t847kbps project gave a mature design of the mentioned decoder. Key features include;
* low power consumption, 17uA@1.8VDC (UMC 0.18um CMOS, can be further reduced per request)
* 50% duty of ETU clock output
* better PAUSE duration, typ. +/-25%@106kbps
* non-return zero decoder output with 1/8 ETU clock period shift from PAUSE signal
* FDT0 and FTD1 available
FPGA codes including PCD stimulus are available. Die samples are also available with 0.18um CMOS (UMC).
Design waves (bmp format, can be displayed by web browser with "open with Firefox or IE, etc", die and die test images also are available in the download section.
